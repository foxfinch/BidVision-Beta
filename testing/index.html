Beta testing checklist.

A quick spin to put BidVision through its paces, plus how to tell us what you find. Five minutes, give or take.

Give these a try

  1. Load a bid sheet (yours, or the sample below).
  2. Set your weekly availability, then tweak a couple of dates.
  3. Rank one filter with Prefer, Allow, or Exclude.
  4. Drag your filters into a priority order.
  5. Switch between Discover and Strict, and watch the list change.
  6. On a phone or tablet, try it on both and see how each feels.

No bid sheet handy? Grab the sample bid sheet and load that instead.

When something is off

Jot three things and we can chase almost anything down: what you did, what you expected, and which device you were on.

  • Easiest: tap the Feedback button inside the app. It opens the report form.
  • Or open the form directly (link below).
  • Prefer to just talk it through? Text us at (215) 688-5012.
  • Screenshots help a lot. Email them to support@bidvision.app.
