BidVision

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026 · Applies to the BidVision beta program

BidVision is a tool for airline flight attendants that reads your monthly bid package and helps you search and filter it. It is built privacy-first: the app does its work entirely on your device. This policy explains, plainly, what that means — and the few places where we do handle some information (mostly when you sign up for the beta).

BidVision is made by FoxFinch Adaptations LLC ("we," "us," "our"), a Pennsylvania limited liability company.

This policy covers the BidVision beta. The beta is free and requires no account. When BidVision later adds paid subscriptions, the features that come with them — such as an account, billing, identity verification, and payment processing — will be explained in an updated policy that we publish before those features collect any new information. Your bid-sheet and schedule data are designed to keep being processed on your device.

The short version

  • Your airline data never leaves your device. Bid sheets are read on your device and are never sent to us or anyone else.
  • The app needs no account and no sign-in to do its job.
  • We never sell, rent, or advertise with your data.
  • When you join the beta, we collect the contact details you give us so we can send your access code and updates.
  • Our website runs no analytics or advertising trackers.

1.The BidVision app

When you load a bid package into BidVision, the app parses and displays it entirely on your device. Your schedule, your trip data, and the PDF itself:

  • are processed locally, on your device;
  • are not transmitted to us, to any server of ours, or to any third party;
  • stay on your device until you remove them (the app's "clear all data" does this).

BidVision needs no account and no internet connection to read and filter your bid sheet. We operate no servers that receive your airline or schedule data — because there are none.

If you choose to send us a screenshot or a bid sheet to help with a support question or a bug report, we will receive what you send and use it only to help you and improve BidVision.

2.The beta program (signing up)

To join the BidVision beta, you fill out a short form on our website. We collect:

  • Name (a first name is fine) and email address — required, so we can send your access code and beta updates;
  • Base and device/platform — so we know who is testing on what;
  • Phone number and group-chat preference — optional, only if you choose to join the tester group chat.

We use this information solely to send your access code and welcome email, send beta-related updates (new builds, instructions, the occasional request for feedback), understand our tester mix, and respond to your feedback. We do not use it for advertising, and we do not sell or share it.

This information is stored in Google Workspace (a Google Sheet and email), and beta emails are sent through Google's services. You can ask us to update or delete your information at any time — email support@bidvision.app.

3.The website

Our website (bidvision.app) is a set of static pages hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not use analytics, advertising trackers, or tracking cookies.

Two ordinary things happen when any website loads:

  • Hosting logs. GitHub, which serves the site, may automatically log standard request data (such as your IP address and browser type) to operate its servers. This is governed by GitHub's privacy practices.
  • Fonts. Our pages use a web font delivered by Google Fonts. To deliver the font, Google receives your IP address. Google's use of it is governed by Google's own policy.

The beta page also stores a small flag in your browser's localStorage to remember that you have unlocked the downloads. This stays on your device and is not sent to us.

4.Mobile beta (iPhone, iPad, Android)

  • iPhone / iPad: the iOS beta is delivered through Apple's TestFlight. When you use TestFlight, Apple collects information (such as your email, device details, and app crash and usage data) under Apple's privacy policy. To invite you, we use the email address that is your Apple ID solely to send your TestFlight invitation.
  • Android: the Android app is a file you download and install yourself. We do not distribute it through the Google Play Store during the beta. The download is served from GitHub (see hosting logs above).

5.Service providers

We keep our toolset small. The companies that process limited information on our behalf, or in serving the site, are:

  • Google — Workspace, Sheets, and Gmail for beta-signup storage and email; Google Fonts for website font delivery;
  • GitHub — website and download hosting;
  • Apple — iOS beta distribution via TestFlight.

Each handles data under its own privacy policy. We do not share your information with anyone else, and we never sell it.

6.Data retention

We keep your beta-signup information for as long as the beta program runs and you are a participant. When the beta ends, or when you ask us to remove you, we delete your contact details from our tester records. Email support@bidvision.app to be removed at any time.

7.Your choices and rights

  • Access, correct, or delete: email support@bidvision.app and we will take care of it. Wherever you live, we will honor reasonable requests about the information we hold.
  • Stop hearing from us: ask to be removed, or reply to any beta email.
  • California residents (CCPA/CPRA): we do not sell or "share" your personal information, and we do not use it for cross-context behavioral advertising. You may request access to or deletion of the information we hold — just email us.
  • Avoiding the web font: because the font is delivered by Google, blocking third-party requests (or using a privacy browser or extension) stops the font from loading while leaving the site fully usable.

8.Security

We use reasonable measures to protect the limited information we hold (for example, access-controlled Google Workspace). No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure, and BidVision is beta software, so we cannot guarantee absolute security. Because the app keeps your airline data on your own device, the most sensitive information never reaches us in the first place.

9.Children

BidVision is a professional tool for working flight attendants and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

10.Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle information, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above. For material changes affecting beta testers, we will also note it in a beta email.
