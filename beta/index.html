⚠ Windows — temporary earlier build. The Windows download is the previous version for now while the newest build finishes. It works fine — it's just one release behind — and we'll email you the moment the current Windows build is ready. (Mac, Linux, and mobile are all on the latest.)
⚠ Windows SmartScreen may show a warning. Click More info, then Run anyway. This is normal for new apps without an extended trust history. You only need to do this once.

Click the Apple menu () in the top-left corner of your screen, then About This Mac.

  • If it says M1, M2, M3, or M4 → choose Apple Silicon
  • If it says Intel (or doesn't mention a chip) → choose Intel

Most Macs from 2020 onward are Apple Silicon. If yours is older than that, it's almost certainly Intel.

On your phone or tablet

You're on a phone or tablet — here's how to install.

 iPhone & iPad

Want BidVision on iOS? Email support@bidvision.app with the email address that's your Apple ID, and we'll add you to TestFlight and send your invite.

iOS runs through Apple's free TestFlight app. While we finish Apple's beta review, we add testers by hand — so your invite may take a day. Full step-by-step is in the install guide below.

🤖 Android phone & tablet Installs from a file (.apk) — not on the Play Store during beta

macOS

BidVision is signed and notarized by Apple, so it opens like a normal app — no scary warnings.

  1. Unzip the downloaded file, then drag BidVision into your Applications folder.
  2. Double-click BidVision. The first time, macOS may ask “BidVision was downloaded from the Internet — are you sure you want to open it?” — click Open. That's it.

If macOS ever says it “can't be opened”: open the Apple menu → System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll to the bottom, click Open Anyway, then confirm with your password. (On macOS Sequoia the old right-click → Open trick no longer works — this is the new path.)

Windows

  1. Run the downloaded installer (.exe).
  2. If SmartScreen shows “Windows protected your PC,” click More info, then Run anyway. This appears because the app is new — it's not a virus warning.
  3. Follow the installer; BidVision opens when it finishes, and lives in your Start Menu from then on.

No Run anyway button? Right-click the .exe → Properties → check Unblock → OK, then run it again.

Linux / Chromebook

  1. Chromebook only: turn on Linux first — Settings → About ChromeOS → Developers → Linux development environment → Set up (one-time, a couple of minutes), then move the .deb into your Linux files folder.
  2. Double-click the .deb and choose Install — or in a terminal run sudo apt install ./BidVision*.deb.
  3. Launch BidVision from your app launcher.

iPhone & iPad (TestFlight)

  1. Email us the address that's your Apple ID (see “On your phone or tablet” above), and wait for the invite email.
  2. On your iPhone or iPad, install the free TestFlight app from the App Store.
  3. Open our invite email on that device and tap Start Testing (or View in TestFlight).
  4. In TestFlight, tap Accept, then Install.
  5. Open BidVision from your home screen. New versions arrive through TestFlight — tap Update when prompted.

Android

  1. Tap Android phone & tablet above to download the .apk file.
  2. Open the downloaded file — from the download notification, or your Files / Downloads app.
  3. Android will ask permission to install from this source — tap Settings, turn on Allow from this source, then go back. (On Android 15 this is granted per-app — allow it for the app you downloaded with, usually your browser or Files app.)
  4. Tap Install, then Open.
  5. To update later, download the new .apk and install it over the top.

Why BidVision?

Your bid sheet has over a thousand sequences. BidVision helps you find the ones that actually fit your life — faster and with more flexibility than anything else out there.

Build your schedule around your life

Mark the days that matter — a wedding, a trip, your kid's recital — and BidVision finds trips that work around them. Or prioritize the trips you like and see which days they fall on. It works either way.

More flexible than what you're using now

BidVision is built for how we actually bid — with more filters, more control, and more ways to search than the current tools offer. Updated, robust, and designed around real crew needs.

Soft filters find hidden gems

Most tools use hard yes-or-no filters that hide everything else. BidVision lets you prefer, allow, or exclude — so trips that almost match still show up, scored and ranked. You might find a sequence you'd never have seen otherwise.

BidVision is a pre-bid research tool — use it anytime to explore your options before the bidding window opens.
