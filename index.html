Find the trips that fit your life—not just the ones you're legal for.

BidVision reads your bid sheet and ranks every trip by what you actually want: the days you can work, the trips you'd trade for, the patterns you avoid. It all runs on your device.

Tell it when you can fly. Once.

Set a weekly pattern and tweak individual dates for the month. BidVision scores every trip against your real availability—by the hour, not just the day—so the trips that fit your life float to the top. Your pattern persists across bids; just adjust the exceptions.

Prefer, Allow, Exclude—a spectrum, not a switch.

On any filter, place each option where it really sits for you. Prefer is your ideal—pull these to the top. Allow is a good one—a lighter nudge up. Leave an option untouched and it stays neutral—no opinion either way. Exclude is a negative—push it down. They're soft by default, so nothing disappears unless you switch to Strict. It's how you'd actually rank your month, turned into a sorted list.

Explore everything, or zero in.

Discover shows all your trips ranked by preference—great for spotting trips you'd have scrolled right past. Flip to Strict when you know exactly what you want, and it hides anything that breaks your rules.

Hunting a specific trade? Filter to the dates that matter.

Set a date range and BidVision surfaces every trip touching those days—the fast way to research ETB and TTS trades around the schedule you're trying to build.

Your data never leaves your device.

No airline data is sent to or stored by BidVision servers. All parsing, filtering, and scoring happens on your device.
