Keep going with BidVision.

Your build reached its expiration date. That's by design—here's how to pick up right where you left off.

Get the latest build

Why builds expire

BidVision is still in beta, and beta builds are set to expire on purpose. It keeps everyone on the newest, safest build instead of running an old one with bugs we've already fixed. So an expiry notice isn't a problem—it's just a nudge to grab the current build. It takes about a minute.

If BidVision says your build expired

  1. Go to bidvision.app/beta.
  2. Enter your access code.
  3. Download the latest build for your computer (Mac, Windows, or Chromebook/Linux).
  4. Install it over your old copy—no need to uninstall first.

Your setup carries over.

Your saved bid sheets, availability, and preferences live in your computer's app-data folder, not inside the app itself—so updating the build leaves all of it untouched. Open the new build and everything's right where you left it.

One more thing—for early adopters

When BidVision goes live, beta testers get founding-member pricing. More on that soon—thanks for helping us build it.
